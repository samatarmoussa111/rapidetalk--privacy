# Privacy Policy for RapideTalk

**Last updated: June 30, 2026**

## Overview

RapideTalk is a language learning app that helps French speakers learn English through daily voice recordings. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

RapideTalk does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

Specifically:

- **Voice recordings** are stored locally on your device only
- **Transcripts and translations** are stored locally on your device only
- **Vocabulary lists** are stored locally on your device only
- **No analytics** or tracking tools are used
- **No accounts** or sign-ups are required
- **No data** is shared with third parties

## Device Permissions

The app requests the following permissions, used only for the described purposes:

- **Microphone**: To record your voice notes
- **Speech Recognition**: To transcribe your French recordings into text

## Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. RapideTalk does not have access to your payment information.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting individual recordings within the app or by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

RapideTalk does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
